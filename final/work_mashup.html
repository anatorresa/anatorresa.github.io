Poster Infographic for DJ Earworm's Summer 2014 Mash-up. Titled: Pop Music Encore, the poster emphasizes repetitive elements in pop music videos by quantifying them: repeating lyrics and memes. It also shows the amount of times a song becomes part of the track and for how long.
