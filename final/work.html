The aim of the Bloom poster was to surprise pedestrians with a tactile experience. The idea was to create a responsive poster that would slowly come to a full bloom by the date of the start of the event. Bloom rethinks the conventional 2D poster by using laser-cutting. Later on, I decided instead to photograph the paper cut images in order to achieve a similar effect and lower the cost of the design.
